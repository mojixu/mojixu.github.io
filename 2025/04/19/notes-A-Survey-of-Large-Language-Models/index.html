notes-A_Survey_of_Large_Language_Models

“The limits of my language mean the limits of my world“

  • statistical language models -> neural language mdoels

  • pre-trained language models(PLMs)被提出，为了与小规模语言模型区分，这部分模型称作large language models(LLM)，随着规模（parameter 和 corpora）增大，其表现出一些小规模语言模型所不具备的能力。

  • language modeling -> task solving language models的转变

Introduction

“read, write and communicate like humans”

  • Language modeling: model the generative likelihood of word sequences, so as to predict the probabilities of future(missing) tokens.

    • Statistical Language Models (SLM): the curse of dimensionality
    • Neural Language Models (NLM): MLP, RNN word2vec -> learning distributed word representations
    • Pre-trained Language Models (PLM): capture context-aware word representations (semantic features). paradiagm-> pretraining ，then fine-tuning (ELMo, BERT)
    • Large Language Models (LLM): Larged (data size or parameter size) PLM ,在解决复杂任务上，相比于small language models有一些额外的能力**(emergent abilities) ** （Chatgpt, LLama,PaLM）

  • 早期language models聚焦于model and generate text data；现在的LLM聚焦于complex task solving

  • 三大挑战：
    • 为什么LLM有用emergent abilities是一个未解之谜
    • LLM需要大量的计算资源
    • 让LLM具有人类价值观和偏好（LLM可能生成暴力、有害内容）
